About Velokraft

Built on a foundation of trust

Velokraft Auto Parts is a European supplier of high-quality auto components, headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia. We serve workshops, dealers, and vehicle owners across Europe with fast, reliable delivery.

We source every part from verified, ISO-certified manufacturers and cross-check against OEM specifications before it reaches you. Our team brings deep automotive expertise to every order.

Whether you're a garage owner looking for a trusted trade partner or an individual after a specific part — Velokraft delivers.
