About Velokraft

Built on a Foundation of Trust

Velokraft Auto Parts is a leading European supplier of high-quality auto components, headquartered in Tallinn, Estonia. We serve workshops, dealers, and vehicle owners across Europe with fast, reliable delivery.

We source every part from verified, ISO-certified manufacturers and cross-check against OEM specifications before it reaches you. Our team brings deep automotive expertise to every order.

Whether you're a garage owner looking for a trusted trade partner, or an individual searching for a specific part — Velokraft delivers.

🏭

Verified Suppliers Only

We partner exclusively with ISO-certified manufacturers and authorized distributors — zero counterfeit risk.

🚚

Europe-Wide Delivery

Fast dispatch from our Tallinn warehouse. Express delivery available across the EU and beyond.

🛡️

Warranty on Every Part

All products carry a minimum 6-month warranty. Many OEM parts come with 12–24 month coverage.
